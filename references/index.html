The piggy at war

For this course I am going to make a pig that can run around, jump and perform some simple attacks and other actions.

Inspiration for this character came from various sources. The primary one being an episode in one of my favourite cartoons called " The Mask" - When pigs ruled the world. After searching some time online I was able to find the episode on YouTube. It was one of my favourite episodes from the entire series and the cartoon itself represents a very important part of my childhood. The pig men appear many times in the episode.
