The Course

This is a survey course of 3D modeling, rendering, and animation technologies, with an emphasis on character development for games and high definition video. There are two major deliverables for the course. One is a fully modeled, shaded and rigged character, integrated into a 3D game environment. The other is a fully rendered 30 - 60 second scene, complete with lighting and animation, output in high definition
